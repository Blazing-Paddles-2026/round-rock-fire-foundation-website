Priority 01

Donor FAQ

Honest answers, not fine print.

Is my gift tax-deductible?

Yes. Gifts are made through trusted giving pathways with Central Texas Community Foundation and Network for Good. You’ll receive a tax-deductible receipt by email after each gift.

How are donations processed?

Online gifts are made through the Central Texas Community Foundation giving pathway in partnership with Network for Good. Your card or bank information is never stored on our site.

What percentage goes to programs vs. overhead?

RRFF is in its founding season, so formal annual reporting will follow after the first completed reporting year. Current giving is made through the Central Texas Community Foundation pathway in partnership with Network for Good.

Can I direct my gift to a specific program?

Yes. The current priority is launching The 1884 Fund. You may also give toward wellness, community events, or where most needed once the giving flow is configured.

How do I cancel or change a monthly gift?

Monthly gifts are processed through Central Texas Community Foundation and Network for Good. You can update or cancel a recurring gift directly through the Network for Good receipt and donor tools that arrive with each contribution.

Does my employer match?

Many do. If your employer offers a matching-gift program, your HR or giving portal can route the match alongside your gift. Reach out to legacy@roundrockfirefoundation.org if you have questions about routing a matching gift.

How can I give in honor or memory of someone?

Add a note in the giving flow with the honoree’s name. We’ll send an acknowledgment card to the family at the address you provide.
